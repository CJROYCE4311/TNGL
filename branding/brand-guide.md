# SG Couples League Branding

Modern course-inspired identity for a member-run couples golf league playing at Sterling Grove Golf & Country Club.

## Design Direction

This revision moves away from generic desert iconography and uses a more realistic Sterling Grove-inspired course scene: White Tank Mountain backdrop, warm low sun, lush fairway, groves of trees, sand bunkers, water, and the clean SG/Couples League lockup.

Research references:

- Sterling Grove official golf page: https://www.sterlinggroveclub.com/golf/
- Sterling Grove Nicklaus Design page: https://www.sterlinggroveclub.com/golf/nicklaus-design
- Nicklaus Design course page: https://nicklausdesign.com/course/sterling-grove-golf-country-club/

## Files

- `sg-couples-league-primary-badge.svg`: primary full-color badge for shirts, website, and printed materials.
- `sg-couples-league-horizontal.svg`: horizontal logo for headers, emails, and wide-format placements.
- `sg-couples-league-ball-mark.svg`: compact two-color mark for golf balls, tees, towels, and small embroidery.
- `sg-couples-league-one-color.svg`: single-ink version for economical printing and embroidery.

## Usage Notes

- All SVGs use transparent backgrounds.
- Use the ball mark anywhere the available print area is smaller than 1.25 inches wide.
- The full-color badge is best for apparel, web, and printed league materials.
- The one-color version is best for embroidery, single-ink golf balls, and simplified production.
- Before final production, ask the printer to convert SVG text to outlines so the lettering stays exact even if their system does not have the same fonts installed.
